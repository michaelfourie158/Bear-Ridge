About Us

Bear Ridge Holdings is a trusted logistics company specializing in the transport of various goods including Timber, Cement, Fertilizer, Coal, and Tile Cement. We pride ourselves on timely and reliable service across multiple destinations.

Founded in 2001, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability in the logistics industry. Our experienced team and modern fleet ensure that we can handle any logistics challenge with ease.

Company Information

  • Founded: 2001
  • Headquarters: Nelspruit, South Africa
  • Owner: Willem Fourie
  • Mission: To provide reliable and efficient transport solutions that exceed our customers’ expectations.
  • Vision: To be the leading logistics provider in South Africa, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
  • Values: Integrity, Reliability, Innovation, and Customer Focus.
