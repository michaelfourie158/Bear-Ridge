About Us

Bear Ridge Holdings is a transport and logistics company based in Nelspruit, South Africa. We specialize in the transportation of goods such as coal, chrome, cement, fertilizer, and timber to various destinations. Our services are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients, providing efficient and reliable transport solutions for a wide range of industries.

Founded in 2001, Bear Ridge Holdings has established itself as a leader in the industry, providing efficient and secure transport solutions to meet the needs of our clients. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time.
